Features

Everything your DZ needs

Built from the ground up for how parachute clubs actually operate. Every feature was designed with real jump day workflow in mind.

Live Load Board
Create loads, assign pilots, add jumpers, and track status from Building through Landed. Reorder loads on the fly for wind holds or instructor scheduling.
Automatic Credit Tracking
Tandem instructor base pay, rig bonuses, camera bonuses, outside video pay, student instructor credits, AFF dual instructor credits, and pilot pay all calculated automatically.
Member Management
Track regular members, students, and walk-ins. Manage waivers, gear repacks, ratings, dues payments, and packing credits all from one profile page.
Excel Reports
Generate formatted Excel reports with date range selection. Seven sheets covering monthly jumps, instructor earnings, pilot activity, gear status, aircraft stats, and dues.
Live Display Board
A dedicated read-only jumper display designed for a second monitor. Updates silently every 10 seconds with no flicker. Jumpers can see their load without coming to the desk.
Automatic Backups
Database backed up automatically every time the app opens and closes. Manual backup downloads in one click. Your data is always protected.
Gear Repack Tracking
Track reserve repack dates for personal and club gear. Members can have multiple rigs. Warnings appear 30 days before repacks are due. Overdue rigs block manifesting.
Fully Configurable
Set your club name, colors, pay rates, waiver validity, default aircraft, and more. Every financial rate is configurable to match your club's agreements.
Emergency Contacts
Searchable emergency contact list visible without admin login. Anyone on the DZ can pull up a contact number instantly when it matters most.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No server setup. No internet connection. No IT department needed. Just double click and go.

1
Copy the folder
Copy the app folder to your DZ computer. Everything is included — no installation wizard needed.
2
Double click to open
Double click Manifest-182.exe. A loading screen appears and the app opens as a standalone window.
3
Configure your club
Set your club name, pay rates, aircraft, pilots, and ticket types. Takes about 15 minutes the first time.
4
Start manifesting
Create a load, assign a pilot, add jumpers. Credits calculate automatically. That is it.
Pay Tracking

Every dollar tracked automatically

Add a jumper to a load and every credit calculates instantly. Remove them and everything reverses. Nothing left unbalanced.

Tandem Instructor
Base pay + personal rig bonus + inside camera bonus. All configurable per your club's rates.
Outside Video
Base pay + club or personal camera bonus. Videographer auto-added to the load when selected.
Student Instructors
Per-ticket instructor credit. Stacks for multiple students. AFF Cat A-D supports dual instructors.
Pilots
Individual credit per load rate set on each pilot profile. Auto-credited on assignment.
Packing Credits
Regular and tandem pack rates. Logged on member profile and added to cash balance automatically.
Credit Transfers
Transfer credits between any two members. Full audit trail with required notes on every transaction.
