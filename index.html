About

Built by a skydiver for skydivers

Manifest 182 started with a simple problem — our club was running the manifest on paper and a spreadsheet, and instructors never quite knew what they had earned at the end of the day.

After a season of manual calculations, missed credits, and lost paperwork, I built Manifest 182 from scratch to solve it properly. Every feature in this app came directly from real jump day experience.

The name comes from the Cessna 182 — the backbone of small club aviation and the plane that started it all for us.

Every dollar your instructors earn is tracked automatically. Every gear repack is watched. Every waiver is enforced. So you can focus on the jump day — not the paperwork.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does it require internet to run? ▼
No. Manifest 182 runs completely offline on your DZ computer. No internet connection is needed to open the app, manifest jumpers, track credits, or generate reports. The only time you need internet is to download the software in the first place.
What happens if the computer crashes? ▼
Your data is saved to disk instantly after every action — there is no save button because it saves continuously. The app also creates automatic backups every time it opens and closes. You can also manually download a backup at any time from the admin panel and save it to a USB drive.
Can we run it on multiple computers? ▼
The app runs on one computer at a time. However a second monitor can display the live jumper board by opening a browser on the same computer and going to the local address. The display board updates automatically every 10 seconds with no flickering.
How does instructor pay work? ▼
When you add a tandem or student to a load, instructor pay is calculated and credited automatically based on the rates you configure in Settings. Tandem instructors earn a base rate plus optional rig and camera bonuses. Student instructors earn a per-ticket rate that you set on each ticket type. AFF Cat A through D support dual instructors who each earn the full credit. All credits reverse automatically if a slot is removed.
What types of jumps does it support? ▼
Manifest 182 supports all common jump types — regular licensed jumpers, walk-in tandem passengers, walk-in students, static line progressions, and AFF Cat A through H. AFF categories A through D automatically show a dual instructor dropdown. You create your own ticket types in the admin panel with custom prices and instructor credit amounts.
Can we customize the pay rates? ▼
Yes — everything is configurable. Tandem base pay, personal rig bonus, inside camera bonus, outside video base pay and camera bonuses, packing pay rates, pilot pay per load, and student instructor pay per ticket type. All rates are set in the admin Settings panel and take effect immediately.
Do we need to install Python or any software? ▼
No. The app comes as a single exe file with everything bundled inside. Just copy the folder to your computer and double click to open. No installation, no Python, no dependencies to manage.
What if we get a new computer? ▼
Copy the entire app folder to the new computer via USB drive — make sure to include the manifest182.db file which contains all your data. Double click the exe and everything is right where you left it. No reinstallation needed.
